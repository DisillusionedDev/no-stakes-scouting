The System Flaw: Half-Space Retraction Chaos

Frankfurt’s tactical identity relies on heavy vertical transitions down the flanks. However, statistics reveal a severe defensive trade-off. When the vertical wave is turned over, their double-pivot is frequently caught too advanced to execute proper rest-defense coverage.

As a result, opposing counters funnel rapidly into the half-spaces between their wide center-backs and advancing wing-backs. Frankfurt ranks in the **bottom quarter of the Bundesliga for opposition transition tracking sequences**, transforming simple loose turnovers into high-value xG concessions.
